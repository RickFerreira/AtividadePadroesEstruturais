Structural Design Patterns

It's a design pattern that defines how classes and objects are organized to form larger structures, facilitating flexibility and code reuse. They help maintain a modular and efficient system architecture.

Adapter

The structural design pattern "Adapter," also known as "Wrapper," is used to enable classes with incompatible interfaces to work together.

Bridge

The "Bridge" pattern is like a two-level system, or a bridge, for organizing a program's code.
